SECTION 1: THE ONLY RULES YOU NEED TO KNOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Gold buyers get a reward that starts high (5%) and decreases over time.
2. Miners (block creators) get a fixed base reward of 0.0003% on every block they mine.
3. The reward for gold buyers will never go below 0.0003% — the same as miners.
4. Everyone wins, but early adopters win more.

A: Yes — starts high, ends at 0.0003%.

Q: Do miners get a reward?
A: Yes — always 0.0003% per block.

Q: Does the miner reward ever decrease?
A: No — it's fixed forever.
